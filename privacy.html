TrekAssistant

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

TrekAssistant ("we," "our," or "us") is developed by MeDig LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how the TrekAssistant mobile application handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

TrekAssistant is a local-first application. All data you enter — including vehicle profiles, maintenance records, expenses, trips, and documents — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal data.

The only information that leaves your device is:

  • Trip community submissions — if you choose to submit a trip to the TrekAssistant community, the trip details (destination, dates, rating, notes) are sent to our review system. Submission is always voluntary and requires your explicit action.
  • Crash reports — anonymized crash data may be collected by Apple through standard iOS mechanisms to help us improve app stability.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not track your usage or behavior within the app
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not display ads

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Photos and documents you add are stored in the app's private storage directory. This data is included in your device's standard iCloud or iTunes backup if you have backups enabled.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

TrekAssistant uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — handles payment processing for the app purchase. We do not receive or store your payment information.
  • OpenWeather API — used to display weather information on the home screen. Your location is not sent; weather is retrieved based on a general region.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the Discover feed content shown in the app. No personal data is transmitted.

5. Children's Privacy

TrekAssistant is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
