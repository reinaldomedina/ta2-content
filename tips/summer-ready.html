Get Ready for Summer — Top 10 Checks After Storage

Don't hit the road without running through this list first.

1
🔴 Critical

Fresh Water System Sanitization

Flush and sanitize the entire fresh water system. Mix 1/4 cup of bleach per 15 gallons of tank capacity, fill the tank, run water through every faucet, let sit 4 hours, then flush completely. Rodents and bacteria love dormant water lines.

2
🔴 Critical

LP Gas System & Detector Test

Turn on LP gas and check all connections with soapy water for bubbles. Test the LP gas detector — press the test button. Check the propane regulator, burners, and water heater. Replace the detector if it's over 5 years old.

3
🔴 Critical

Roof & Seals Inspection

Inspect every inch of the roof for cracks, tears, or separated seals around vents, AC units, and antennas. A single compromised seal can allow water damage that costs thousands. Re-apply lap sealant wherever needed.

4
🔴 Critical

Tires — Age, Pressure & Condition

Check tire age (manufactured date on sidewall — replace at 6 years regardless of tread), inflate to sidewall specs cold, and inspect for sidewall cracking, bulges, or flat spots from storage. Stored tires dry out and crack even with good tread.

5
🟡 High

Battery Check & Charge

Test battery voltage and charge fully. Check terminals for corrosion — clean with baking soda solution. A battery left discharged over winter sulfates and may not hold full capacity anymore. Load-test if you suspect it's weak.

6
🟡 High

Brakes & Wheel Bearings

For trailers, inspect brake magnets and drums. Spin each wheel and listen for grinding. If you haven't repacked wheel bearings within 12,000 miles or 2 years, do it now before summer travel.

7
🟡 High

Slide-Outs & Awning

Extend all slide-outs and inspect seals and wiper blades. Lubricate slide mechanisms with appropriate lubricant. Extend the awning fully and check for tears, mildew, or debris. Clean and re-treat fabric if needed.

8
🟡 High

Air Conditioning

Run the AC for 15 minutes and confirm it's cooling. Clean or replace the air filter — a clogged filter kills efficiency in summer heat. Check the roof gasket around the AC unit for separation.

9
🔵 Normal

Pest Inspection

Check every cabinet, compartment, and the underbelly for rodent evidence — droppings, chewed wiring, or nesting material. Mice can destroy wiring harnesses and AC ducting during storage. Check engine compartment on motorhomes.

10
🔵 Normal

All Systems Functional Check

Run through every system: refrigerator (both modes), microwave, water heater, furnace, all interior lights, exterior lights, TV antenna, and all slide and leveling controls. Better to find problems in the driveway than 500 miles from home.
