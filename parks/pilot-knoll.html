Beautiful park with great lake access — and you have the city of Highland Village right next door for shopping, restaurants, and anything you need. About

Pilot Knoll Park sits on the western shore of Lake Lewisville in Highland Village — one of the most convenient campgrounds in the DFW metroplex. The park spans 65-85 acres depending on lake levels and features the Poindexter and Hickory Creek equestrian trails. Wildlife is exceptionally tame here — turtles, ducks, and herons are common sightings at the water's edge. Shopping, dining, and entertainment are minutes away.
