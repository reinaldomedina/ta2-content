Twin Coves Park

Full Hookups Cabins Available Reservations Required Hiking & Biking No Shade on Sites
🚐 RVer's Tip

Outstanding staff and one of the best-maintained parks in North Texas. Amazing hiking and biking trails including the 22-mile Northshore Trail. Sites have no shade but the location and amenities make up for it. Book well in advance — weekends fill fast.

About

Twin Coves is a hidden gem on the north shore of Grapevine Lake, operated by the Town of Flower Mound. After a $4 million renovation it now offers resort-level amenities with a natural setting. The 22.5-mile Northshore Trail is one of the most popular hiking and mountain biking trails in North Texas. Shopping, restaurants, and entertainment in Flower Mound are minutes away.
