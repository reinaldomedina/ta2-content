About the Lake

Lake Texoma sits on the Oklahoma-Texas border and is known as the "Striper Capital of the World." With over 89,000 acres of water, it offers world-class striped bass fishing, boating, and water sports. Deer, turkeys, hawks, and waterfowl are abundant around the campground.
