About

Lake Ouachita is one of the cleanest lakes in Arkansas with 975 miles of pristine shoreline. Located on the edge of the Ouachita National Forest, the park is about 1 hour 20 minutes west of Little Rock and a short drive from the historic Hot Springs district — making it the perfect base for exploring the Spa City while enjoying world-class camping. Wildlife is abundant including mallards, herons, and deer.
