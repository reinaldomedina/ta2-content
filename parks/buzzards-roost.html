About

Buzzard's Roost is one of nine campgrounds in Lake Murray State Park, Oklahoma's first and largest state park. The campground sits on the southeast end of Lake Murray with direct lakefront access — sites are literally steps from the water. The iconic Tucker Tower offers panoramic views of the lake and a fascinating history. The clear blue-green water here is unlike most Oklahoma lakes.
