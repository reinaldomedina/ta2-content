About

Burns Run West is the larger of the two Burns Run campgrounds on Lake Texoma, with miles of trails surrounding the lake and significantly more shade than its eastern counterpart. Section C in particular offers a stunning tree canopy. Known as the "Playground of the Southwest," Lake Texoma provides world-class striped bass fishing and endless water recreation.
